# Grey literature

An overview of sources of grey literature/things that might not be indexed by archives of scientific literature

* [AJOU Open Repository](https://repository.ajou.ac.kr/)
* [EThOS](https://ethos.bl.uk)
* [CiNii](https://ci.nii.ac.jp)
* [RISS](https://www.riss.kr/index.do)
